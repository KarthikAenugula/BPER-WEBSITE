Instructions:
  • Prospective candidates are required to apply online, carefully following the comprehensive instructions provided when online applications are announced, concurrently with the release of the examination notification.
  • The online application form can be completed and submitted through the Commission's web portal by the specified deadline for application submissions, as indicated in the Examination Notice.
  • It is strongly recommended that applicants meticulously review the detailed instructions provided by the Commission at the time of inviting online applications. This ensures that their applications remain free from avoidable issues leading to rejection.
  • Upon the successful completion of both Part-I and Part-II of the online application process, candidates are required to submit their unique RID (Registration ID) number and date of birth. Subsequently, they should click the "SUBMIT" button to finalize the entire application procedure.
