About us

Board of Professional Education and Research

Board of Professional Education and Research is also known as BPER. BPER is an autonomous Professional Institution, Recognised by Government of India. BPER offers Non Formal Higher Education for the working professional through different modes of learning to achieve their Graduation without disturbing their career / job. BPER offers various Membership Level Examinations for the different level of students. BPER offers Technician / Diploma Membership Examination (TMBPER), Graduateship / Associate Membership Examination (AMBPER), Post Graduateship / Chartered Membership Examination (CMBPER) which is Equivalent to 3y Diploma, UG Examinations, PG Examinations accordingly. BPER offers RPL mode, WILP mode, Blended mode of Examinations which student can appear according to credibility of their qualification.
